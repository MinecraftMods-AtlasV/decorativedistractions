# Decorative Distractions

**Decorative Distractions** is a Minecraft mod that adds a variety of decorative blocks and items, and a few mechanics for your builds. From colorful crystal variants to light panels and tintable blocks.

## Features

### Amethyst Variants
The mod introduces 15 new crystal variants (based off amethyst) inspired by real-world minerals. Each variant comes with its own set of blocks and items:
- **Crystals:** Selenite (White), Carnelian (Orange), Eudialyte (Magenta), Celestite (Light Blue), Citrine (Yellow), Hiddenite (Lime), Thulite (Pink), Smokey Quartz (Gray), Magnesite (Light Gray), Plancheite (Cyan), Azurite (Blue), Tiger's Eye (Brown), Malachite (Green), Realgar (Red), and Jet (Black).
- **Blocks:** Each variant includes a full set of blocks: Amethyst-like blocks, Budding variants, Clusters, and Buds of various sizes.
- **Slabs & Stairs:** Added for both the new variants and the vanilla Amethyst Block.

### Stone Sets
Expand your masonry options with new smooth and smooth brick variants for existing stone types:
- **Stone Types:** Diorite, Andesite, Granite, Deepslate, Tuff, and Blackstone.
- **Variants:** 
    - **Smooth:** A sleek, polished look.
    - **Smooth Bricks:** A refined, yet still smooth brick pattern.
- **Complete Sets:** Each variant includes Stairs, Slabs, Walls, Buttons, and Pressure Plates, because texturing is important.

### Tintable Blocks
Add a splash of custom colour to your world with Tintable Blocks. These blocks can be dyed any colour and will even change the colour of grass placed on top!
- **Blocks:** Tinted Cobblestone, Tinted Stone, and Tinted Grass Blocks.
- **Stairs & Slabs:** Tintable variants are also available for stairs and slabs.
- **Colourful:** Each block can be dyed any colour, just like leather armour.

### Light Panels
Decorative and functional lighting solutions that respond to redstone and can be togged by hand.
- **Variants:** Available in Copper, Iron, and Gold frames, each in all 16 Minecraft dye colors (48 total combinations).
- **Modal Toggle:** Clicking with an empty hand will toggle the state, turning the panel on or off and changing the redstone behaviour.

### Mechanics & Tools
- **Mortar and Pestle:** A new crafting tool used to grind crystal shards into **Crystal Dust** and then into **Pigments** for another way to obtain dyes. 
- **Paintbrush:** Another crafting tool used to convert regular blocks into their tintable variants
- **Pigments:** Pigments are an alternative to dyes, and can be crafted from the **Crystal Dust** allowing for a more compact and renewable dye obtaining system.
